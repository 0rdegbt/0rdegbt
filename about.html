Hi! Nice to meet you!

My name is Orde Gilbert Wiguna, I was born on the 17th of October 2003 in Kabupaten Tangerang, Banten, Indonesia. Currently, I'm a second semester Informatics student of Universitas Multimedia Nusantara's 21st generation.

Aside from studying, there are other things I do, like:

I play a lot of video games

Video games have been my main source of entertainment since I was little, starting from PSP games, to present day PC/Laptop games. I play various genres of video games, RPG, Strategy, Rougelike, Platformer, even Card Games. The picture on the left are title screens of the video games I play actively nowadays, like Genshin Impact, Skul: The Hero Slayer, Arknights, and Yu-Gi-Oh: Master Duel.

Getting into data analysis

When I'm not playing video games or doing my assignments, I like collect game statistics, create multiple different scenarios, and see the outcome. Currently, this is limited to video games such as damage calculation. However, I do believe that this would be a handy skill to have later in the future.

Graphic design is my passion

With over 4 years of experience using Photoshop and other editing softwares, I have grown to love creating, modifying, and editing pictures/videos. So far, I've created mockup movie posters, wallpapers, even original illustrations.

Not just digital art, but also paintings

Besides creating and editing artworks digitally, I also have experience in painting. Specifically using acrylic paints and watercolor paints. However, I don't do this often as the cost of canvases and the paints themselves can get quite expensive.

Assembling plastic models

Another hobby I've recently started enjoying is making plastic model kits. While they can get extremely expensive, I enjoy the process of building and assembling them. On top of that, the end result is also satisfying to look at.
